Static Try-on SDK

Through a short video recording users turning head right and left horizontally, users can easily try virtual glasses on and see the selfie on the mobile in real-time, and can select frame models according to personal preferences. It provides glasses sellers with unprecedented product exhibition and promotion efficiency, and improves SKU exposure. Prerecord can also provide the best try-on experience for myopia users.

Dynamic Try-on SDK

With the Dynamic SDK of TopGlasses™, camera gather real-time head images of users, and wear the virtual glasses on their face. Users can adjust their head poses as they want and observe the try-on results from different angles simultaneously on screen. Dynamic try-on makes user experience more intuitive and interesting, as well as provides a better support to glasses stores try-on and vending machine try-on services.

Cosmetic Contact Lenses Try-on SDK

The SDK enables users to upload a personal portrait, and display the feature of eye pupil after trying different cosmetic contact lenses, which benefits users to choose their favorite products, increases the exposure and promotion rate of products, and improves repurchase rate.

Size Measurement SDK

With unique and precise facial dimensional measurement technology, TopGlasses™ easily obtains millimeter-precision pupil distance, and other crucial sizes, efficiently creating facial type analysis and advices. The qualitative analysis of users’ facial type is the precondition of personalized product recommendation

About

Founded in February 2015, Topplus™ is a leading computer vision technology startup in China and the world. It has over 15 years of tech accumulation and experience in the field of computer vision, augmented reality and photogrammetry. Since our establishment, we have developed TopGlasses™ virtual try-on sdk, TopUav™ tracking and image stabilization system in succession. Besides, Topplus™ has signed the agreement with Shanghai Industrial Technology Institute, primarily researching and developing intelligent streetlight in the “Smart City" CV module project. In addition, Topplus™ has signed the cooperation agreement with State Grid Corporation of China, researching and developing the UHV transmission line CV inspection robot project.
As the first startup with academician workstation nationwide, Topplus™ has set up three laboratories in stereoscopic vision and augmented reality, visual navigation and tracking, big data and deep learning. Topplus™ has built sound cooperative relations with many top universities and research institutions at home and abroad.
  In the computer vision community, Topplus™ actively advocates dialogue, expands cooperation and promotes win-win. As the platinum sponsor of VALSE 2016 Seminar, Topplus™ will continue to participate and support top conferences and entrepreneurial practice in the field of computer vision and machine learning.
